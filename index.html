About

I’m Rengga Maulana — an Indonesian Software Engineer currently based in Bogor, West Java.

As an experienced software engineer with over 2 years in the field, I am passionate about technology and believe that a programmer is always learning. I specialize in developing and managing responsive websites using languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, and PHP, along with modern frameworks like JQuery, React JS, Vue JS, Angular, and Ionic. I am also skilled in backend technologies like Laravel and database management with MySQL. Known for my strong analytical skills and attention to detail, I have refined these abilities in my recent role. I adapt quickly to new environments, a skill demonstrated not only in my career but also through organizing technology seminars and workshops during my academic years.
